Our foundation

Vision, mission & values

Vision

A well-informed public

A Silti Woreda where every citizen and institution has timely, reliable access to government information, supporting a transparent and accountable administration.

Mission

Reliable, proactive communication

Ensuring citizens have timely access to accurate government information while promoting professional excellence, research, innovation, and transparent public communication. We work to meet the information needs of government institutions and the public by building a reliable, efficient, effective, and proactive communication system.

Values

What guides our work

  • Accuracy and integrity in every release
  • Transparency with the public we serve
  • Professional excellence and continuous learning
  • Innovation in how information reaches citizens
  • Responsiveness to community needs
